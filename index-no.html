Hungary opposition wins two-thirds majority ending Viktor Orbán's 16-year rule
Péter Magyar's Tisza party secured 138 seats in Hungary's 199-seat parliament on April 12, removing Orbán who had blocked EU aid to Ukraine and served as Putin's main ally in Europe. The result clears the way for a 90 billion euro loan to Ukraine and ends Hungary's adversarial role inside the European Union.
