What this is

News with no agenda and nothing to hide.

What this is

I made Rumbo.wtf for myself. I got tired of the noise — the clickbait, the outrage loops, the ads dressed up as news. But I also can't afford to be completely disconnected. Knowing roughly what is going on is just part of functioning — having a conversation, understanding why things cost more, not being completely lost at the table.

So I wanted the minimum effective dose. Enough to grasp the big picture. As little attention as possible, but not zero.

Why it exists

I want to free up more time for the things that actually matter: real conversations, being more present, being occasionally awed by the world rather than anxious about it. News used to be something only the privileged had time for. Now it has been engineered to be addictive, which is not the same as useful. Rumbo.wtf is a means, not an end. Read it in five minutes, close it, go live.

How we see the world

We approach the news the way an observer arriving on Earth for the first time would, without allegiance to any country, bloc, religion, or ideology. There is no home team. Everything is described by what it does and what interests it serves, not whether it is right. If forces killed people, we say which forces.

How it is made

Each edition is generated daily by an AI language model, currently Claude by Anthropic, though the model may change as we evaluate which produces the most precise and neutral output. No single AI company has a permanent seat at the editorial table.

AI makes mistakes. So does every other news source. Journalists, editors, and owners are not perfect either. If every major outlet is reporting the same false story, it will likely make it into Rumbo too. That is not a flaw unique to AI; it is a flaw in how information moves through the world.

What Rumbo.wtf can promise is this: no deliberate spin, no agenda, no commercial pressure to keep you reading longer than is good for you. No ads, ever. The prompt used to generate each edition is shown below. Nothing is hidden.
