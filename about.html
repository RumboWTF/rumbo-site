What this is

I made RumboRumbo — Spanish and nautical for heading, course, direction. Where things are going. because I got tired of noise: clickbait, outrage loops, ads dressed as news. Rumbo gives the minimum effective dose — the big picture in five minutes. Then you close the tab.

How Rumbo sees the world

Rumbo approaches the news like an outside observer. No home team, no allegiance to country, bloc, religion, or ideology. Events and actors are described by what they do and who they serve, not whether they are right.

How it is made

Each edition is generated daily by an AI language model (currently Claude by Anthropic). The model may change as Rumbo evaluates output quality.

AI makes mistakes, like any news source. What Rumbo removes is the rest: no deliberate spin, no agenda, no ads, no pressure to keep you reading. The prompt is public. Nothing is hidden. Sources vary by day and story — no fixed list, no single outlet relied upon.

Testing phase. Rumbo is a work in progress. Errors will occur. Use it as a starting point, not a final word.
