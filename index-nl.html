President Marcos declared a state of national energy emergency, making the Philippines the first country in the world to do so. The country imports 98 percent of its oil from the Middle East, leaving 115 million people critically vulnerable as closure of the Strait of Hormuz endangers supply.

India launches world's largest census with caste enumeration for first time in nearly a century
India began counting its population in a year-long census costing 1.24 billion dollars, during which three million officials will survey 1.4 billion people.
